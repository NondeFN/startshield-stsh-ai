# StartShield-STSH AI Demo

## Overview

This demo showcases how StartShield-STSH AI evaluates startups, MSMEs, and innovation-driven enterprises using an AI-powered Trust Infrastructure framework.

The platform combines multiple specialized AI agents to generate trust assessments, investment readiness evaluations, risk intelligence, insurance readiness insights, and AfCFTA expansion recommendations.

---

## Demo Objective

Demonstrate how AI can support entrepreneurs, investors, incubators, accelerators, universities, and ecosystem builders by providing structured business intelligence and decision-support tools.

---

## Demonstration Workflow

### Step 1

User submits startup information.

↓

### Step 2

AI agents independently evaluate the startup.

↓

### Step 3

Agent outputs are consolidated.

↓

### Step 4

TrustScore formula is applied.

↓

### Step 5

Unified Trust Intelligence Report is generated.

↓

### Step 6

Recommendations are provided.
