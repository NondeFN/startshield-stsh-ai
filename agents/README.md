# StartShield-STSH AI Agents

## Overview

The StartShield-STSH AI platform is powered by a collection of specialized AI agents designed to strengthen trust, improve investment readiness, reduce risk, and accelerate sustainable growth for startups, MSMEs, innovation ecosystems, and entrepreneurs across Africa.

Each agent performs a specific function while contributing to a unified Trust Infrastructure framework.

---

## Core Agents

### 1. TrustScore Agent
Evaluates startups and enterprises using a structured trust assessment framework.

Functions:
- Trust scoring
- Ecosystem credibility assessment
- Governance evaluation
- Operational readiness analysis
- Growth potential assessment

Output:
- TrustScore (0-100)
- Risk Rating
- Readiness Assessment

---

### 2. Venture Validation Agent
Assesses the viability and sustainability of startup ideas and business models.

Functions:
- Business model validation
- Market opportunity assessment
- Competitive analysis
- Customer problem-solution fit
- Startup readiness evaluation

Output:
- Venture Validation Score
- Market Viability Assessment
- Recommended Improvements

---

### 3. Funding Agent
Supports startups in identifying appropriate funding pathways.

Functions:
- Funding readiness assessment
- Investor readiness evaluation
- Grant opportunity recommendations
- Capital strategy guidance
- Funding pathway mapping

Output:
- Investor Readiness Score
- Recommended Funding Sources
- Capital Access Roadmap

---

### 4. Risk & Insurance Agent
Provides insurance-first risk intelligence and resilience assessments.

Functions:
- Operational risk analysis
- Business continuity assessment
- Insurance gap identification
- Compliance risk evaluation
- Enterprise resilience scoring

Output:
- Risk Profile
- Insurance Readiness Report
- Mitigation Recommendations

---

### 5. AfCFTA Expansion Agent
Supports startups and MSMEs seeking regional growth opportunities under the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA).

Functions:
- Market expansion analysis
- Cross-border readiness assessment
- Trade opportunity mapping
- Regulatory awareness support
- Regional growth recommendations

Output:
- AfCFTA Readiness Score
- Expansion Potential Assessment
- Recommended Target Markets

---

## Unified Trust Infrastructure

Together, these agents create a comprehensive AI-powered trust infrastructure that enables:

- Startup Trust Assessment
- Investment Readiness Evaluation
- Venture Validation
- Risk Intelligence
- Insurance Readiness
- AfCFTA Expansion Planning
- Ecosystem Development Support
