# StartShield-STSH AI Architecture

## Overview

StartShield-STSH AI is an AI-powered Trust Infrastructure platform designed to support African startups, MSMEs, entrepreneurs, innovation ecosystems, incubators, accelerators, investors, and development partners.

The platform leverages multiple specialized AI agents orchestrated through a unified intelligence framework to evaluate trust, investment readiness, business viability, risk exposure, insurance readiness, and AfCFTA expansion potential.

---

## Architecture Objectives

The architecture is designed to:

- Assess startup trust and credibility
- Improve investment readiness
- Support venture validation
- Identify business and operational risks
- Promote insurance-first resilience
- Facilitate AfCFTA market expansion
- Generate actionable recommendations

## TrustScore Framework

The platform calculates a unified TrustScore using weighted factors:

Trust Score =

(Business Viability × 30%)

+ (Investment Readiness × 25%)

+ (Team Strength × 15%)

+ (Market Opportunity × 15%)

+ (Compliance Readiness × 15%)

Outputs include:

- TrustScore (0–100)
- Risk Rating
- Investor Readiness Level
- Funding Path Recommendations
- Growth Potential Assessment
- AfCFTA Expansion Potential
